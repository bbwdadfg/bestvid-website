## Intro Script

BestVid is a multi-model AI video and image website built for creators, marketers, agencies, and production teams.

The simplest way to understand BestVid is this:
it is not trying to be just one more thin AI landing page.
It is built to be a clearer front door into modern AI creative production.

Instead of pushing users toward a single model, a single gimmick, or a single workflow,
BestVid is designed around choice.
Choice of models.
Choice of creative direction.
Choice of workflow.
And choice of how to move from an idea to a usable output with less friction.

---

## What BestVid Does

BestVid focuses on AI video generation and AI image generation.
That includes text-to-video, image-to-video, and static image creation for marketing, social content, product visuals, thumbnails, posters, and brand assets.

The website is structured to help users understand which models are worth attention,
what kinds of outputs each model is better at,
and how different creative goals connect to different production paths.

This matters because serious users rarely want only one model.
They want flexibility.
They want comparison.
They want speed.
And they want outputs that can actually be used in content, campaigns, and product storytelling.

---

## Why the Multi-Model Position Matters

One of the central ideas behind BestVid is that not every model is good at the same thing.

Some models are better for cinematic motion.
Some are better for dynamic scenes.
Some are stronger for polished image output.
Some are better for rapid iteration.

BestVid highlights platforms and model families such as Sora, Kling, Veo, Seedance, Seedream, Nano Banana, Wan, PixVerse, and related tools.

The point is not simply to list names.
The point is to help visitors understand fit.
Which model fits which task.
Which workflow fits which goal.
And which path is more practical when the user is trying to produce something real.

## Who BestVid Is For

BestVid is built for several types of users.

It is for creators who need speed and variation.
It is for marketers who need ads, product videos, thumbnails, and campaign visuals.
It is for agencies that need to test multiple creative directions for different clients.
It is for product teams and growth teams that need launch assets and fast visual experiments.
And it is for founders and indie makers who need output quickly without building a heavy internal process.

In short,
BestVid is for people who care less about AI hype,
and more about useful visual production.
